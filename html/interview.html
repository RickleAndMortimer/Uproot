Interview

Description:

You get seen by recruiters, now it's time for them to see if you are the right candidate.

  • Practice/rehearse your presentation to yourself or someone you know.

  • Prepare relevant questions for the interviewer.

  • Research the job listing and company.

  • If you are a teenager who doesn't have job experience, you can instead mention that you have responsibilities for the family such as taking care of a young sibling.

  • Wear proper attire regardless if it is virtual.

  • Be on time!

  • Be prepared to answer the typical questions such as what are your strengths and weaknesses.

  • Anticipate the interviewer's concerns and questions.

  • Be respectful towards the interviewer and thank them for their time.

Best of luck!
