Resume and LinkedIn

Description:

The written paper showcases your experience, achievements, and knowledge to recruiters.

  • Should look plain with little to no visuals on a resume to get through companies' applicant tracking system (ATS). Many applicants' resumes do not reach a human as they fail to get through the robots that cannot even read your resume.

  • Try to quantify things as they can be easier to see by recruiters.

  • Be concise and only list things that would make your resume look better.

  • Update LinkedIn and connect with people by writing in the note of how you are interested in their experience in certain fields so you have more meaningful connections.

  • If looking for a job, enable #OpenToWork so it shows on your profile picture.
