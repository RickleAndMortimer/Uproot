Spending / Saving Money

Description:

You should know how to manage your money as efficiently as possible.

The 50 / 30 / 20 Rule:

  • 50%: On needs such as bills, groceries, utilities, etc.

  • 30%: On wants such as hobbies, eating out, etc.

  • 20%" In savings such as saving accoutns, investments, assets, etc.

Needs can be easily changed, however, the remaining amount for wants and savings should have a similar ratio of 3:2 respectively.

Additionally:

  • Before spending on wants and putting money away into savings besides a savings account, 3-6 months of needs should be allocated in case of emergencies.

  • 1 month's savings * number of months expected for emergency.
