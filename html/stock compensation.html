Stock Compensation

A way that companies use stocks as an incentive to reward employees that support the company.

Taxes must be paid based on the price of the current share when received.

Vesting

Allows employees to purchase a predetermined number of shares at a set price in a set duration of time.

For example: If the predetermined number of shares is 10 for $10 with a timeframe of 3 years, they can purchase those 10 shares for $10 within the 3 years for that price regardless of the increase.

Restricted Stock Units (RSU)

Employees receive shares after working a certain amount of years and meeting performance goals.
