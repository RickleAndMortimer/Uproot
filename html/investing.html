Stock Investments

Description:

When you invest, you build the assets that will eventually return you with more money.

Compounding Interest:

A person should invest as soon as they turn 18 to take advantage of compounding interest. The money you contribute will make profits, and those profits reinvested will net you more profit, recycling over a long period.

For example:

If you contributed $1,000 to invest with an interest rate of 10% per year, your first year profit would be $100, increasing your account balance to be worth $1,100.
