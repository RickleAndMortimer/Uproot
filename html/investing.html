Stock Investments

A person should invest as soon as they turn 18 to take advantage of compounding interest

Compounding Interest:

The money you contribute will make profits, and those profits reinvested will net you more profit, recycling over a long period.

For example:

You contributed $1,000 to invest with an interest rate of 10% per year.

The first year profit would $100, increasing your account from $1,000 to $1,100.
